Brewlingo Pourover Baseline

TL;DR
  • Roast level > everything else (it dictates temp, grind, agitation).
  • Bean density (altitude) = temperature driver.
  • Processing sets solubility (anaerobic/natural = lower temp).
  • Agitation is the #1 cause of over-extraction in home pour-overs.
  • Ratio is the least sensitive variable — 1:15–1:17 almost always works.
